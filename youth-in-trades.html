Youth in Trades

The Country Car Show is more than a great day out — it helps the next generation of mechanics, restorers, and technicians get their start.

Partial proceeds from the Country Car Show support the ACSS Automotive Program at Aldergrove Community Secondary School, helping local high-school youth in Aldergrove, BC take their first steps toward a career in the automotive trades.

What your support makes possible

Hands-on automotive training gives students real, marketable skills and a clear path into a well-paid, in-demand profession. By supporting the program, our show helps keep that opportunity open for students who might not otherwise have access to it.

Where the funds help

  • Training & certifications — support for course materials and skills training. [Specifics to confirm with the club.]

How to get involved

Every car that rolls in and every sponsorship makes a difference. Here are three ways to help support Youth in Trades:

  • Attend or enter the show — come out on Sunday, April 26, 2026, or pre-register your vehicle.
  • Donate or sponsor — businesses and individuals can support the program directly; see how on our sponsors page.
  • Spread the word — share the show with anyone who loves classic cars and wants to back local youth.
