Welcome to the Central Fraser Valley Chapter

We are a community of vintage automobile enthusiasts who gather to share the cars, the stories, and the open road. Whether you own a classic, you're working on one, or you simply love seeing them, you are welcome here.

About Our Club

The Central Fraser Valley Chapter is a chapter of the Vintage Car Club of Canada, dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and enjoyment of classic and collector automobiles. Our members range from seasoned restorers to first-time owners, all united by a love of these cars and the friendships they bring.

Throughout the year we organize tours, picnics, shows, and informal drives. New members are always welcome — you do not need to own a vintage vehicle to join.

Membership dues support club operations and are payable annually. Please contact us using the information at the bottom of the page if you would like to learn more or attend a meeting as a guest.
