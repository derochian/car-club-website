12th Annual

Country Car Show 2026

Sunday, April 26, 2026 · 9 AM – 3 PM

Hosted by the Central Fraser Valley Chapter of the Vintage Car Club of Canada at the Aldergrove Community Secondary School. Free admission for spectators — bring the family.

Pre-Register Your Vehicle

Event Details

The Country Car Show is a one-day vintage and classic car show held each spring in Aldergrove, British Columbia. Partial proceeds support the ACSS Automotive Program — helping local youth enter the automotive profession.
