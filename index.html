About the Show

The Country Car Show is a one-day vintage and classic car show held each spring in Aldergrove, BC. Here is a taste of what the day has in store.

  • Trophies

    Trophies awarded at 2:30 PM, plus dash plaques for the first registered entries.

  • Raffle & 50/50

    A raffle and a 50/50 draw run throughout the day — great prizes to win.

  • Food & Entertainment

    Food and entertainment on site to keep everyone happy from morning to afternoon.

  • Family-Friendly

    All ages welcome, with free admission for spectators. A great day out for the whole family.

Partial proceeds support the ACSS Automotive Program — helping local youth enter the automotive profession. Learn how the show supports youth in trades →
